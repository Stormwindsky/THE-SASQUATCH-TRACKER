# SUBJECT-001: Cascade Mountain Giant

**Status:** Threatened (High Risk)
**Region:** Pacific Northwest, USA
**Size:** 2.4m - 2.9m

## Description
The Cascade Giant is known for its reddish-brown fur and distinctive whistling call. It is highly aggressive during mating season.

## Hunting Protocol
> **Warning:** Never approach without a thermal scanner.
1. **Scent Masking:** Use pine-needle extract.
2. **Attraction:** Use wood-knocking techniques (3 rapid strikes).
3. **Capture:** Not recommended. Observe from a distance of 50m.
